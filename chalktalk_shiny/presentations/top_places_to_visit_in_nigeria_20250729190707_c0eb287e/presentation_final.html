Top Places to Visit in Nigeria

Nigeria’s Most Amazing Destinations

Lagos - The Commercial Capital

Nigeria’s vibrant megacity

  • Victoria Island - Modern business district
  • Tarkwa Bay Beach - Island paradise
  • National Theatre - Cultural hub

Abuja - The Federal Capital

Modern planned capital city

  • Aso Rock - Iconic 400m monolith
  • National Mosque - Architectural marvel
  • Millennium Park - Urban oasis

Calabar - Cultural Crossroads

Historic city of festivals

  • Calabar Carnival - Africa’s biggest street party
  • Calabar Museum - Colonial heritage
  • Cross River National Park - Rainforest sanctuary

Yankari National Park

Nigeria’s premier wildlife sanctuary

  • 50+ mammal species including elephants
  • Wikki Warm Springs - Natural heated pools
  • Safari drives and photography

Osun-Osogbo Sacred Grove

UNESCO World Heritage Site

  • Sacred sculptures and artwork
  • Annual Osun Festival celebration
  • Ancient forest sanctuary

Jos Plateau

Nigeria’s temperate highland region

  • Jos Wildlife Park - Highland animals
  • National Museum - Nok sculptures
  • Hiking trails and rock formations

Benin City Heritage

Ancient capital of Benin Empire

  • National Museum - Benin Bronzes
  • Oba’s Palace - Living royal tradition
  • Ancient city walls - Historic fortifications
